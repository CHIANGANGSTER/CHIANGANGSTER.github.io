## Issues

- **[Section 8.1 vs Section 16 D8]**: The CmdK trigger contract contradicts the resolved decision. Section 8.1 requires `/` to open the modal, while D8 says `Cmd+K` / `Ctrl+K` is the only trigger.
  - Why it matters for planning: `cmdk.js` implementation and acceptance tests will differ depending on whether `/` is required.
  - Suggested resolution: Remove the `/` trigger from Section 8.1, or revise D8 and acceptance criteria to explicitly include it.

- **[Section 11 S1 deliverable 10 vs Section 13 S1 acceptance]**: The seeded stub entry is required to appear as a second `/workflows/` card, but the same deliverable says it "must not appear in production-relevant indexes" while it lives in `kb.json`, the production source for hub cards and global search.
  - Why it matters for planning: Implementers need to know whether the stub is production-visible fixture data, dev-only fixture data, or filtered out of search/hubs.
  - Suggested resolution: Choose one contract: either make the second entry production-visible with `status: planned`, or add an explicit fixture/dev flag plus filtering rules and update the acceptance criteria accordingly.
